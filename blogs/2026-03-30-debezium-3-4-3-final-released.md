Debezium 3.4.3.Final is now available, bringing targeted improvements and important bug fixes, with a focus on the Oracle connector. This release delivers a meaningful performance improvement on connector restart, resolves several Oracle-specific parsing and compatibility issues, addresses a multibyte UTF-8 encoding bug in the PostgreSQL connector, and improvements to the Informix connector.
